Our Story

Small trust.
Serious purpose.

Moosa Trust UK was founded on a simple conviction: that ordinary people, giving what they can, can change extraordinary things. We are a lean, values-driven trust — no big offices, no expensive overheads, no corporate machinery. Just three trustees who believe deeply in Amanah (trust), Ihsan (doing things well), and Rahma (mercy to all people).

That belief shapes everything. We use Gift Aid to cover running costs — or trustees cover them personally — so that when you give £10, £10 reaches someone who needs it. Not £7. Not £8.50. All of it.

Where Your Money Goes

Three causes.
One purpose.

Every donation is directed into one of three areas where the need is greatest and the impact is lasting.

Thirst Relief

Clean Water & Wells

785 million people live without clean water. A well doesn't just quench thirst — it ends hours of daily labour, cuts child illness, and keeps girls in school. Every well you fund is a Sadaqah Jariyah flowing long after you're gone.

See our first project
Bright Futures

Education & Schools

A child without education is a future locked away. We fund classrooms, supplies, and teacher training in underserved communities — because Iqra was the first word revealed, and knowledge is the most enduring gift.

Support education
Hardship Relief

Poverty & Emergency Aid

When crisis strikes — flood, famine, or sudden loss — families need help now, not in six months. We move quickly, act with dignity, and stay for the long term. Because the Prophet ﷺ said: the best of people are those most beneficial to others.

How We Work

Not just words.
How we actually operate.

Amanah — Trust

You'll never wonder where your money went. Every donation is tracked, every project is documented, and every penny is accounted for. We report back because you deserve to know.

Ihsan — Excellence

We don't cut corners on the people we serve. We choose partners carefully, vet projects rigorously, and hold ourselves to the standard of someone who knows Allah is watching. Because He is.

Rahma — Mercy

Need has no religion or nationality. We serve every human being who is suffering — with the same dignity, care, and urgency — because that is what our faith demands of us.
