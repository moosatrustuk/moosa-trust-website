About This Project

Our first step toward lasting change

This is where Moosa Trust UK began — with a simple goal: fund one water well for one community. £500 was raised from family and community donations, and the full amount has been donated to commission a branded Moosa Trust UK water well in Sri Lanka.

The well is being delivered through Benefit Mankind, an established charity with experience and infrastructure on the ground. As a new trust, we work with established organisations like this to leverage their expertise and economies of scale — ensuring your donations are delivered effectively and efficiently. As we grow and build our own knowledge, we hope to deliver projects directly in the future, insha'Allah.

Once the well is complete, this page will be updated with photographs, location details, and a full impact report showing exactly how your generosity translated into clean water for real families.

Implementing Partner

Benefit Mankind — Water Programme

Our well is being commissioned through Benefit Mankind's water programme, which delivers clean water infrastructure across communities in South Asia. They provide end-to-end delivery, local oversight, and photographic evidence of completion.
