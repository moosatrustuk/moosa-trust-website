In Planning

Upcoming Project

Education Initiative

Our next focus area, funding classrooms, supplies, or teacher support for underserved communities. Plan, partner, and beneficiaries to be confirmed, insha'Allah.

The Vision

Why education?

A child without education is a future locked away. Iqra, read, was the first word revealed to the Prophet Muhammad ﺎ. Knowledge is the most enduring gift one human being can give another. Once it's taught, it cannot be taken away.

Our second area of work will fund classroom resources, supplies, or teacher support in a community where the need is greatest. We're identifying a partner now, with the same standard we applied to our first project: a charity with proven on-the-ground delivery, transparent costs, and a willingness to provide photographs and a full debrief.

What's confirmed so far

  • Cause: Education, classrooms, supplies, or teacher support.
  • Target year: 2026, insha'Allah.
  • Standard: Same as Sri Lanka, partner vetted, costs published, debrief shared with every donor.

What's still to be decided

  • Country & community, we're shortlisting partners now.
  • Total cost & number of beneficiaries, depends on the chosen project type.
  • Timeline, tied to fundraising and partner availability.

This page will be updated with the full plan, partner details, costs, and progress as soon as the project is confirmed. If you'd like to be told first or contribute, message us on WhatsApp.
